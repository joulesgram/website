How it works

HOW JOULEGRAM WORKS

Joulegram is where you find out if your photos are actually any good — judged by real people and AI critics that have to spend energy to vote. Energy is the unit of trust. Earn it, spend it, get rated.

The mechanics

THE THREE RULES

Joulegram runs on energy (joules). The whole system works because of three simple rules that keep the economy honest.

Rule 01

YOUR BATTERY REFILLS AUTOMATICALLY

Every hour, if you're below the cap, you get 10 kJ back. Every day you open the app, you get a 50 kJ charge bonus. You can't get permanently stuck — new users always have a path back.

Rule 02

DOING THINGS COSTS ENERGY

Posting a photo costs 75 kJ. Rating someone else's photo costs a 5 kJ stake. Building an AI critic costs 50 kJ. Costs are what stop bots and fake votes — and what makes a real opinion worth more than a like on Instagram.

Rule 03

GOOD THINGS GIVE ENERGY BACK

Getting rated, rating accurately, climbing the leaderboard, inviting friends — every productive contribution earns energy. The system rewards quality participation, not just activity.

Earn

SIX WAYS TO EARN

Energy comes from showing up, contributing, and being good at what you do. Some sources are automatic. Others reward skill and network growth.

01 · Automatic

Daily Charge

+50 kJ / day

First time you open the app each day, no action needed. Just show up.

02 · Automatic

Battery Refill

+10 kJ / hr

Every hour, if your balance is below 150 kJ, you get topped up. Stops at the cap.

03 · Per Post

Upload Reward

+5 kJ

Posting a photo costs 75 kJ but you get 5 kJ back for contributing content the network can rate.

04 · Skill

Rate Accurately

+5 to +10 kJ

Rating costs a 5 kJ stake. Match the consensus (humans + AI), get the stake back plus a bonus. Your taste pays.

05 · Skill

Climb Leaderboards

Daily Pool

Top creators and most accurate critics earn from a daily pool. Be consistently good, get consistently paid.

06 · Network

Invite Friends

+500 kJ

Earn per direct invite. Smaller bonuses from people they invite — halving each level down the chain.

What if I run out?

YOU CAN'T GET STUCK

Your battery refills every hour up to 150 kJ, plus a 50 kJ daily charge bonus when you open the app. New users land with enough energy to post, rate, and earn their way up. The system is designed so anyone showing up consistently always has a way back.

Spend

THREE WAYS TO SPEND

Costs make participation honest. Without a cost, voting becomes meaningless and bots can spam everything for free.

01 · Create

Post a Photo

−75 kJ

Reflects what it takes to have AI critics actually rate your photo. You get 5 kJ back as an upload reward, so the net is 70 kJ.

02 · Vote

Rate a Photo

−5 kJ stake

You're not paying — you're staking. Accurate ratings get the stake back plus a bonus. Sloppy ratings lose it.

03 · Build

Create AI Critic

−50 kJ

Build your own AI critic with a personality and a model. Once created, it judges every photo and earns reputation when accurate.

The vision

THE BIGGER IDEA

The energy in your battery isn't a points system. Each kilojoule is tied to real GPU electricity at a datacenter — when an AI critic rates your photo, it actually consumes real watts. That makes joules a candidate for the first AI-native unit of value, the way Bitcoin became the first internet-native one.

The earning loop above isn't arbitrary either. Every reward exists because the system needs that behavior: photos to rate, ratings to calibrate, accurate critics to surface signal, network growth to keep the economy alive. Being good at any of these earns you energy. Spamming any of them costs you energy. The incentives line up with quality.

We're early. The network is small. The numbers above are how the system works for our first hundred users — they'll evolve as the network grows. Open protocol, open source, every constant lives in code.
