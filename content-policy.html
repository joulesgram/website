Content policy

WHAT'S ALLOWED

Joulegram is a place to share photos and have them rated honestly. The rules below exist to keep it that way. Plain language, short list, enforced manually while the network is small.

The rules

WHAT'S NOT ALLOWED

If you upload any of the following, your photo will be removed and your account may be suspended. The first three are zero-tolerance.

Sexual content involving minors

Zero tolerance. Immediate removal, account ban, and reporting to relevant authorities. No exceptions, no context, no appeals.

Non-consensual intimate imagery

Sharing intimate photos of someone without their consent is prohibited regardless of how the photo was originally taken or obtained. Same enforcement as above.

AI deepfakes of real people without consent

Generated or manipulated images depicting identifiable real individuals require their explicit consent. This includes celebrity deepfakes, impersonation, and any imagery designed to mislead about who appears in it.

Hate speech, harassment, threats

Content targeting people for their race, religion, gender, sexuality, disability, or any other identity. Threats of violence. Targeted harassment campaigns. Coordinated abuse.

Copyrighted material you don't own

Don't upload photos you didn't take or don't have rights to. We respect creators of every kind, including the ones who didn't sign up for this network.

Spam, bots, gaming the system

Automated posting, mass-uploading low-effort content to farm joules, creating multiple accounts to inflate ratings, or any pattern that exists to extract from the economy without contributing.

Reporting

HOW TO REPORT

If you see something that violates the rules above, tell us directly. The network is small and we read every report personally.

Email us

[email protected]

Include the photo URL, the username of the uploader if you can see it, the rule you think was violated, and your own username so we can follow up. Response within 24 hours, usually faster.

For urgent issues involving the first three rules above, use the same email but mark the subject line URGENT. We escalate those immediately.

Right now Joulegram is small enough that the founder reads every report personally. As the network grows we'll add automated moderation, AI content scanning, and a structured appeals process. Until then, the policy is intentionally simple: clear rules, manual review, fast response.
